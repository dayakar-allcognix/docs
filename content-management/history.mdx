## Overview

The History feature provides comprehensive tracking and management of all your AI-generated content. Access your complete generation history, search through past creations, re-download images, and track generation details to optimize your creative workflow.

    - Verify account synchronization
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Search Problems">

## Best Practices

<Accordion title="History Management">
- Regularly review and organize your generations
- Use descriptive prompts for easier searching
- Save important generations to favorites
- Clean up unwanted generations regularly
- Export important content for backup
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Search Optimization">
- Use specific keywords in prompts
- Tag generations with relevant categories
- Use filters to narrow down results
- Save successful prompt patterns
- Organize content by project or theme
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Storage Efficiency">
- Monitor storage usage regularly
- Archive old generations when needed
- Use appropriate resolution settings
- Compress images when possible
- Delete unsuccessful generations
</Accordion>
