## How to Use

### Step 1: Prepare Your Sketch

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sketch Quality">
    Create a clear, well-defined sketch:
    - Use dark, bold lines
    - Ensure good contrast
    - Include key details and proportions
    - Avoid overly complex compositions
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Digital Preparation">
    Scan or photograph your sketch:
    - High resolution (at least 1024x1024)
    - Good lighting and contrast
    - Clean background (white preferred)
    - Supported formats: JPG, PNG
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Step 2: Upload and Configure

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload Sketch">
    Drag and drop or click to upload your sketch file
    - Maximum file size: 10MB
    - Recommended resolution: 1024x1024 to 2048x2048
    - Ensure sketch is clearly visible
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Aspect Ratio">
    Choose dimensions that match your intended use:
    - **Square (1:1)**: Social media, portfolio
    - **Landscape (16:9)**: Presentations, banners
    - **Portrait (9:16)**: Mobile content, posters
    - **Custom**: Set specific dimensions
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Step 3: Write Enhancement Prompt

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Architectural Prompts">

    **Interior Spaces:**

  <Accordion title="Fashion Prompts">

    ```
    Transform packaging sketch into realistic product packaging, premium finish, commercial photography
    ```

  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 4: Generate and Refine

1. Click **Generate Image** to start processing
2. Wait for AI interpretation (20-40 seconds)
3. Review the realistic transformation
4. Use **Regenerate** for variations
5. **Save** your favorite results

## Quality Optimization

<Accordion title="Best Practices">

- **Details**: Include key structural elements
- **Resolution**: Minimum 1024x1024 pixels
