## How to Use

### Step 1: Upload Your Image

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Image">
    Upload the image you want to upscale:
    - Supported formats: JPG, PNG, WebP, TIFF
    - Maximum file size: 20MB
    - Minimum resolution: 64x64 pixels
    - Recommended: 256x256 to 1024x1024 pixels
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Image Preview">
    Review your uploaded image:
    - Check current resolution and quality
    - Identify areas that need enhancement
    - Ensure image is not corrupted
  </Step>
</Steps>
