## How to Use

### Step 1: Upload Your Image

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Image">
    Upload the image you want to edit:
    - Supported formats: JPG, PNG, WebP
    - Maximum file size: 10MB
    - Recommended resolution: 512x512 to 2048x2048
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Image Preview">
    Review your uploaded image:
    - Check image quality and composition
    - Identify areas to edit
    - Ensure good contrast and detail
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Step 2: Create Your Mask

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Masking Tool">
    Choose the appropriate masking tool:
    - **Brush Tool**: Freehand selection
    - **Rectangle Tool**: Rectangular areas
    - **Lasso Tool**: Custom shapes
    - **Magic Wand**: Similar color areas
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Mark Areas to Edit">
    Carefully select the areas you want to modify:
    - Use precise strokes for detailed work
    - Adjust brush size as needed
    - Use zoom for fine details
    - Preview selection before proceeding
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Step 3: Write Your Prompt

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Object Removal">
    **Simple Removal:**
    ```
    Remove the object, fill with background
    ```
    
    **Context-Aware Removal:**
    ```
    Remove the person, fill with natural background, seamless blending
    ```
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Background Completion">
    **Natural Background:**
    ```
    Complete the background, natural landscape, realistic details
    ```
    
    **Specific Background:**
    ```
    Fill with beach background, ocean view, tropical setting
    ```
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Creative Addition">
    **Add Elements:**
    ```
    Add a beautiful sunset sky, warm colors, dramatic clouds
    ```
    
    **Artistic Enhancement:**
    ```
    Add magical elements, glowing effects, fantasy atmosphere
    ```
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 4: Configure Settings

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Blending Mode">
    Control how new content blends with existing image:
    - **Seamless**: Natural blending with surroundings
    - **Subtle**: Minimal changes, preserve original
    - **Creative**: Artistic interpretation
    - **Realistic**: Photorealistic results
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Detail Level">
    Specify the level of detail in the filled area:
    - **Low**: Basic filling, faster processing
    - **Medium**: Balanced detail and speed
    - **High**: Maximum detail, longer processing
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Style Consistency">
    Ensure consistency with the original image:
    - **Match Original**: Maintain original style
    - **Enhance**: Improve while maintaining style
    - **Transform**: Apply new artistic style
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 5: Generate and Refine

1. Click **Generate** to start in-painting
2. Wait for AI processing (20-60 seconds)
3. Review the filled/edited result
4. Use **Adjust Mask** to refine selection
5. **Regenerate** for different interpretations
6. **Save** your final result

## Prompt Writing Strategies

### Effective In-Painting Prompts

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Removal Prompts">
    **Format**: `Remove [object], fill with [replacement]`
    
    **Examples:**
    - "Remove the car, fill with natural landscape"
    - "Remove the person, fill with background continuation"
    - "Remove the text, fill with matching surface"
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Addition Prompts">
    **Format**: `Add [element], [style], [context]`
    
    **Examples:**
    - "Add beautiful flowers, natural garden setting"
    - "Add modern furniture, contemporary interior"
    - "Add dramatic sky, sunset colors"
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Enhancement Prompts">
    **Format**: `Enhance [area], [improvement], [style]`
    
    **Examples:**
    - "Enhance background, natural lighting, realistic details"
    - "Improve sky, dramatic clouds, cinematic atmosphere"
    - "Complete landscape, lush vegetation, natural colors"
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Quality Optimization

### Input Image Requirements

<Accordion title="Best Practices">
- **Resolution**: Use high-resolution images (1024x1024+)
- **Quality**: Avoid heavily compressed images
- **Contrast**: Ensure good contrast and detail
- **Composition**: Clear subject and background separation
- **Format**: JPG for photos, PNG for graphics
</Accordion>

### Masking Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Selection Accuracy">
    - Be precise with mask boundaries
    - Include enough context
    - Avoid cutting through important details
    - Use appropriate brush sizes
    - Preview selection before generating
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Edge Handling">
    - Use soft edges for natural blending
    - Consider feathering for smooth transitions
    - Maintain edge detail where important
    - Avoid hard edges unless intentional
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
