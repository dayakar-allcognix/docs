## Overview

Profile Management provides users with complete control over their account information, subscription details, usage statistics, and personal settings. Access your account overview, manage your subscription plan, track usage patterns, and customize your Omni Photo Studio experience.

## Account Overview

### Profile Information

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Personal Details">
    Manage your personal information:
    - **Display Name**: How you appear to other users
    - **Username**: Unique identifier (cannot be changed)
    - **Email Address**: Primary contact and login
    - **Profile Picture**: Upload and manage avatar
    - **Bio**: Short description about yourself
    - **Location**: Optional location information
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Account Status">
    View your account information:
    - **Account Type**: Free, Pro, or Enterprise
    - **Member Since**: Account creation date
    - **Last Login**: Most recent activity
    - **Account Status**: Active, suspended, or pending
    - **Verification Status**: Email and phone verification
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Usage Statistics

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Generation Statistics">
    Track your AI image generation activity:
    - **Total Generations**: Lifetime image count
    - **This Month**: Current month's activity
    - **Daily Limit**: Remaining generations today
    - **Favorite Tools**: Most used AI tools
    - **Success Rate**: Successful generations percentage
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Content Library">
    Manage your created content:
    - **Saved Images**: Bookmarked favorites
    - **Generation History**: All created images
    - **Shared Content**: Publicly shared images
    - **Downloads**: Downloaded image count
    - **Storage Used**: Current storage consumption
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Best Practices

<Accordion title="Profile Optimization">
- Keep profile information current and professional
- Use a clear, recognizable profile picture
- Write an engaging bio that describes your interests
- Set appropriate privacy levels for your content
- Regularly review and update your preferences
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Subscription Management">
- Monitor your usage to optimize plan selection
- Take advantage of annual billing discounts
- Review billing history regularly
- Keep payment information updated
- Understand plan limitations and features
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Security Maintenance">
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Use strong, unique passwords
- Regularly review login activity
- Keep contact information current
- Monitor account for suspicious activity
</Accordion>
