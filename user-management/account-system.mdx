## Overview

The Account System provides secure user registration, authentication, and profile management for Omni Photo Studio. Users can create accounts using email/password or Google OAuth, manage their profiles, and access personalized features across the platform.

## Registration & Login

### Account Creation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Email Registration">
    Create an account with email and password:
    - Enter valid email address
    - Create secure password (8+ characters)
    - Choose unique username
    - Accept terms and conditions
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Google OAuth">
    Sign up using Google account (recommended):
    - Click "Sign in with Google"
    - Authorize Omni Photo Studio
    - Automatically import profile information
    - Instant account creation
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Email Verification">
    Verify your email address:
    - Check email for verification link
    - Click verification link
    - Complete account setup
  </Step>
</Steps>
