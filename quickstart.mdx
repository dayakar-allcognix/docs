## Welcome to Omni Photo Studio

Follow this quickstart guide to create your first AI-generated image and explore the platform's powerful features.

## Step 1: Create Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up">
    Visit [Omni Photo Studio](https://omniphotostudio.com) and click "Sign Up"
    
    **Registration Options:**
    - Email and password
    - Google OAuth (recommended)
    - Username validation included
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verify Your Account">
    Check your email for verification link
    - Click the verification link
    - Complete your profile setup
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Your Plan">
    Select a subscription plan or start with free credits
    - **Free Tier**: Limited generations per day
    - **Pro Plan**: Unlimited generations + premium features
    - **Enterprise**: Custom solutions for teams
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 2: Explore AI Generation Tools

<Steps>
  <Step title="Text to Image">
    Navigate to the **Text to Image** tool
    
    **Try this prompt:**
    ```
    A serene mountain landscape at sunset with a crystal clear lake reflecting the peaks
    ```
    
    **Features to explore:**
    - Voice input (microphone icon)
    - Style selection (realism, artistic, etc.)
    - Aspect ratio customization
    - Quality settings
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Image to Image">
    Upload an existing image and transform it
    
    **Perfect for:**
    - Style transfer
    - Photo enhancement
    - Creative manipulation
    - Background replacement
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Sketch to Image">
    Upload a hand-drawn sketch
    
    **Great for:**
    - Architectural visualization
    - Fashion design concepts
    - Product mockups
    - Art creation from rough sketches
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 3: Manage Your Content

<Steps>
  <Step title="View Generation History">
    Access your **History** tab to see all generated images
    - Re-download previous creations
    - View generation details
    - Search and filter results
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Favorite Images">
    Use the **Save** button on any generated image
    - Organize by categories
    - Quick access to favorites
    - Export capabilities
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Explore Community">
    Visit the **Explore** section
    - Discover trending content
    - Like and interact with others
    - Copy prompts from community images
    - Download community creations
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 4: Advanced Features

<Steps>
  <Step title="In-Painting Tool">
    Upload an image and use the masking tool
    - Select areas to edit or remove
    - Use voice input for prompts
    - Fill missing parts intelligently
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Image Upscaler">
    Enhance low-resolution images
    - Choose scale factor (2x, 3x, 4x)
    - Preserve original quality
    - Batch processing available
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Analytics Dashboard">
    Monitor your usage
    - Track generation statistics
    - View credit consumption
    - Export usage reports
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Pro Tips for Better Results

<Accordion title="Writing Effective Prompts">
- Be specific and descriptive
- Include style keywords (photorealistic, artistic, minimalist)
- Mention lighting conditions (soft lighting, dramatic shadows)
- Specify composition (close-up, wide shot, aerial view)
- Add quality modifiers (high resolution, detailed, professional)
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Optimizing Image Quality">
- Use high-resolution source images for Image to Image
- Ensure good contrast in sketches for Sketch to Image
- Select appropriate aspect ratios for your use case
- Experiment with different AI models (V1 vs V2)
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Managing Credits Efficiently">
- Preview generations before finalizing
- Use lower quality settings for experimentation
- Save successful prompts for reuse
- Monitor usage in analytics dashboard
</Accordion>
